# Privacy Policy **Last updated:** June 2026 ## Introduction This Privacy Policy explains how HomeScope ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our AI-powered property analysis service, including our website (https://www.tryhomescope.com) and browser extension ("HomeScope"). We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). **This Privacy Policy is provided in compliance with Google Chrome Web Store requirements for browser extensions that access user data.** ## Supported Websites HomeScope is designed to work with the following property listing websites: - **realestate.com.au** (Australia) - **Zillow** (United States) The extension will only access content on these websites when you explicitly click "Analyze" on a property listing page. We do not access or collect data from any other websites. ## Information We Collect We collect the following types of information: ### Property Listing Data When you request a property analysis, we may collect: - Property address - Listing price and price history - Property details (beds, baths, square footage, year built) - Listing descriptions and text content - Property images and image URLs - Page URL of the listing - AI-generated analysis results and reports ### Account Information - Email address - Authentication data and session tokens - User ID - Subscription plan (Basic or Full) - Credit/point balance and purchase history ### Technical Data - IP address - Browser type and version - Device information - Extension version - Operating system ### Usage Data - Analysis history (for Full plan users) - Feature usage patterns - Timestamps of analysis requests ## Browser Extension Data Collection Our browser extension collects data only through **user-initiated actions**: 1. **User-Initiated Analysis** - The extension only accesses property listing content when you actively click "Analyze" on a supported property page. No data is collected automatically or in the background. 2. **Image Collection** - When you request an analysis with image support: - We programmatically access the photo gallery on the listing page - Images are collected solely for AI-powered visual analysis - Images are sent to our AI processing service and not permanently stored after analysis 3. **No Background Tracking** - We do not track your browsing activity, monitor pages you visit, or collect data when you are not actively using the analysis feature. 4. **Local Storage** - Authentication session data (access tokens) is stored locally in your browser's `chrome.storage.local`. This data remains on your device and is automatically cleared when you sign out or uninstall the extension. ## AI Processing To generate analysis results, the following data may be sent to third-party AI service providers: - Property listing text and descriptions - Property images (for Full plan analyses) - Analysis parameters you select **AI Service Providers:** We use OpenRouter and/or OpenAI as our AI processing providers. These providers process your data solely for the purpose of generating the requested property analysis. **Data Use Limitation:** Your property data is used ONLY to generate the requested analysis. We do not use your data for: - Advertising or marketing purposes - Building user profiles - Training AI models (unless explicitly disclosed by the AI provider) - Any purpose unrelated to HomeScope services ## Data Storage ### Basic Plan (No Account Required) - Analysis is performed without saving your history - No property data, images, or results are stored on our servers - Only anonymous usage metrics may be collected ### Full Plan (Logged-in Users) When you create an account and use the Full plan, we store: - Your account information (email, user ID) - Analysis history with property details - Property images and AI analysis results - Subscription and purchase history **Storage Location:** Data is stored on secure Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit. **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your personal information. ## Third-Party Services We share your data with the following third-party service providers: | Service | Purpose | Data Shared | |---------|---------|-------------| | **Supabase** | Database, authentication, and backend services | Account info, analysis history, property data | | **OpenRouter** | AI model routing and processing | Property text, images (Full plan) | | **OpenAI** | AI language model processing | Property text, images (Full plan) | | **Paddle / Stripe** | Payment processing (Merchant of Record) | Transaction data, email (for payments only) | | **Vercel** | Website hosting and edge functions | Technical logs, usage data | Each third-party service provider is contractually bound to handle your data only for the specified purposes and in accordance with their own privacy policies. ## Data Sharing and Disclosure ### What We Do NOT Do We are committed to protecting your privacy. HomeScope does NOT: - **Sell your personal data** - We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes - **Use data for advertising** - Your data is not used for targeted advertising or ad profiling - **Read unrelated websites** - We only access content on realestate.com.au and Zillow when you actively use the Analyze feature - **Access passwords or cookies** - We do not read, collect, or store your passwords, banking credentials, or cookies from any website - **Monitor browsing history** - We do not track, log, or monitor websites you visit outside of the HomeScope analysis feature - **Background surveillance** - No data is collected in the background; all collection is user-initiated ### When We May Share Data We may disclose your information only in these circumstances: - **Legal Requirements** - To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests - **Protection of Rights** - To detect, prevent, or address fraud, security issues, or technical problems - **Business Transfer** - In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice to users) ## Your Privacy Rights ### Under Australian Privacy Principles You have the right to: - **Access** - Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you - **Correction** - Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information - **Deletion** - Request erasure of your personal information (subject to legal retention limits) - **Opt-out** - Object to direct marketing (we do not engage in direct marketing) - **Complaint** - Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) ### How to Delete Your Data You can delete your data through the following methods: **1.
