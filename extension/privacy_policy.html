# Privacy Policy **Last updated:** March 2026 ## Introduction This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our rental property analysis service, including our website and browser extension ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). ## Information We Collect We collect the following types of information: - **Account Information**: Email address and authentication data when you sign up - **Property Data**: Listing photos, descriptions, and property details that you provide or that are accessed from supported property listing pages when you request an analysis - **Usage Data**: Information about how you use our service, including analysis history - **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, and device information ## Browser Extension If you use our browser extension, additional information may be processed as follows: - The extension only accesses property listing content when you actively click "Analyze" - No data is collected in the background - The extension may read visible content on the current listing page, including text and images, solely to generate the requested analysis - The extension does not track your browsing activity outside supported property listing pages ## Chrome Extension Permissions Our browser extension uses the following Chrome permissions, each with a specific purpose: ### Permissions - **`storage`** – Stores your authentication session (access tokens, user info) locally in your browser (`chrome.storage.local`). This allows you to stay signed in between sessions. Session data is only transmitted to our servers when making authenticated API requests and is never accessed remotely by us. - **`tabs`** – Used for three essential functions: - Detecting when you're viewing a property listing on supported sites (Realestate.com.au) - Opening the OAuth login page in a new tab during sign-in - Enabling secure communication between the extension side panel and the property page via `chrome.tabs.sendMessage` - **`sidePanel`** – Allows the extension to run in Chrome's side panel (sidebar), providing a seamless analysis experience alongside the property listing page without interrupting your browsing. ### Host Permissions The extension requests access to these specific domains: - `https://www.realestate.com.au/*` – To read property listing content **only when you click "Analyze"** - `https://*.tryhomescope.com/*` – For authentication and API communication with our servers - `https://*.supabase.co/*` – For secure authentication services provided by Supabase **Important:** These permissions are strictly limited to the purposes described above. The extension does not access or collect data from these sites unless you actively use the extension's features on a supported property listing page. ## Image Collection Process When you request a property analysis, we collect listing images through the following process: 1. **User-Initiated** – Image collection only begins after you explicitly click "Analyze" 2. **Gallery Access** – We programmatically open the photo gallery viewer (PhotoSwipe) on the listing page 3. **Systematic Capture** – We navigate through all gallery slides to collect every available listing image 4. **AI Processing** – Images are sent to our AI service for visual analysis 5. **Temporary Storage** – Images are processed server-side and not permanently stored after analysis completion **You maintain full control:** You can cancel the analysis at any time by closing the gallery. No images are collected without your explicit action, and the gallery closes automatically after collection finishes. ## How We Use Your Information We use your information for the following purposes: - To provide rental property analysis services - To generate AI-based insights and reports based on listing data - To manage your account and provide customer support - To improve and optimise our service (using aggregated, anonymised data) - To communicate with you about updates and support - To comply with legal obligations ## AI Processing To generate analysis results, property-related data may be processed by third-party AI service providers (for example, via OpenRouter). This data is used only to generate the requested analysis and is not used by us for advertising, profiling, or unrelated purposes. ## Data Storage and Security ### Local Storage (Your Browser) - Session tokens and user preferences are stored in your browser's `chrome.storage.local` - This data remains on your device and is automatically cleared when you sign out or uninstall the extension - We do not have remote access to this local storage ### Server Storage - Analysis results and account data are stored on secure Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit - Property listing data is retained to provide the service and maintain your analysis history - We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure ## Data Sharing and Disclosure We do **not** sell your personal information. We may share your information only with: - **Service providers** who assist in operating our platform (hosting, customer support, analytics) - **AI processing providers** used exclusively to generate your analysis results - **Payment processors** (e.g., Paddle as Merchant of Record) to handle transactions and refunds - **Law enforcement or regulatory bodies** when required by law or court order If your payment is processed by Paddle, Paddle's privacy policy and consumer terms also apply to payment-related data. ## Your Rights (Australian Privacy Principles) Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), you have the right to: - **Access** – Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you - **Correction** – Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information - **Deletion** – Request erasure of your personal information (subject to legal retention limits) - **Opt-out** – Object to direct marketing (we do not engage in direct marketing) - **Complaint** – Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) ### Extension-Specific Rights In addition to the above, you can exercise control over extension-related data at any time: - **Clear local session data** – Sign out from within the extension or uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data - **Delete analysis history** – Request deletion through the extension interface (coming soon) or by contacting us - **Disable the extension** – Turn off or remove the extension at any time via Chrome's extension management page (`chrome://extensions/`) - **Request complete data deletion** – Contact our support team to delete your account and all associated data ## Cookie and Tracking Policy **Our browser extension does NOT use cookies, trackers, or any form of user monitoring.** - We do not set, read, or modify any third-party cookies - We do not track your browsing activity across websites - We do not embed analytics trackers or advertising identifiers in the extension - Supabase authentication cookies (if any) are managed entirely by Supabase's auth service, not by our extension - All communication is via direct API calls using your session token ## Data Retention We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations: - **Local session data** – Stored in your browser until you sign out or uninstall the extension - **Analysis history** – Retained on our servers until you delete your account or request deletion - **Property data** – Retained only as long as necessary to maintain your analysis history and provide service - **Technical logs** – Automatically deleted after 30 days - **Account data** – Retained until you request deletion or we terminate the service (with advance notice) ## Children's Privacy Our service is not directed at children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it from our systems. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
